About Us

Lawyers and clients are finding it increasingly difficult to engage valuation experts who are demonstrably independent and genuinely expert in their field. ​ St James Valuation was created to meet this demand, and moreover, to deliver a truly bespoke client centric approach with the partner and senior team actively involved on each and every engagement.

Partners

Steve Taylor FCA MEWI

Steve Taylor FCA MEWI, with over 25 years experience most notably leading the EY valuation practice in both the UK and EMEIA, we have set out to be the first choice for lawyers and private clients.

Steve has acted for the top legal firms around the world, been deposed in the US, given evidence in the UK high courts, acted for governments, private clients, entrepreneurs, coporates and private equity. Restructuring

Steve was the lead valuation partner at EY interfacing with the restructuring practice. He was regularly involved in complex IBRs giving his opinion on the feasibility of restructuring plans and the valuation of business pre and post. ​​

Significant restructuring and value break analysis includes TXU, MG Rover, Corus MYTravel, Northern Rock, Crest Nicholson and Peacocks. He advised the Cypriot government on the administration of its national carrier. Recently involved in the restructuring of the French retail group IKKS. ​​

He advises directors on their fiduciary duties when contemplating the sale of assets in the context of the potential for subsequent challenge of being an 'undervalue' transaction. ​

Steve has worked with the most major restructuring law firms including Akin Gump, Linklaters and Herbert Smith.
