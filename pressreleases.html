Steve Taylor joins Global Advisory Experts

22 October 2020

St James Valuation is proud to announce that founder and senior partner Steve Taylor has been invited to join the Global Advisory Experts group as part of an agreement that will see him as the exclusive valuation expert for the group in the UK.

The group, which includes over 2000 members from 140 countries, are the premier guide to leadinga advisory experts in the world. The organisation distinguishes itself by including only one exxpert in each specialism and country. Therefore all appointments are made with stringent recruitment criteria.

Steve Taylor is the founder and senior partner at St James Valuation. His experience ranges from working in M&A at investment banks to running a Big 4 valuation business in a career spanning thirty years.
